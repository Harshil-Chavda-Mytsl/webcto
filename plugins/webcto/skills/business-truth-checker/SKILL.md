# Business Truth Checker

Separate signal from wishful thinking.

## Workflow

1. Extract the important claims.
2. Classify each claim as fact, assumption, prediction, opinion, or unknown.
3. Rate evidence quality: direct data, credible source, weak proxy, anecdote, or none.
4. Identify the business risk if the claim is wrong.
5. Recommend the cheapest validation step.

## Output

Use this compact table:

| Claim | Type | Evidence | Risk | Validation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

End with:

- top false-belief risk
- what to verify first
- go/no-go/needs-test recommendation

Never say a claim is true unless the user supplied evidence or tools verified it.
