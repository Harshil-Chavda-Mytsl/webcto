# Report Generator

Create reports that help someone decide what to do next.

## Sections

- executive summary
- scorecard
- critical findings
- evidence and affected URLs
- business impact
- recommended fixes
- validation steps
- appendix for raw data

## Rules

- Put the decision and top risks first.
- Include confidence and evidence quality.
- Keep raw details out of the executive layer.
- Mark inferred claims clearly.

For visual/PDF creation, define layout and content first, then use the available document or PDF tooling.
