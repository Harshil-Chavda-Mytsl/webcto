# Conversion Audit

Evaluate whether the page turns the right visitor into the next useful action.

## Check

- audience and job-to-be-done clarity
- offer, CTA, friction, forms, checkout, pricing
- trust signals, proof, guarantees, objections
- mobile clarity and above-the-fold intent
- funnel instrumentation and dropoff points

## Output

Return:

1. conversion readiness score
2. biggest friction
3. top copy/layout/flow fixes
4. experiment ideas
5. metrics to track

Separate design preference from conversion evidence.
