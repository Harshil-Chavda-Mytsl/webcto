# SEO Audit

Audit SEO with evidence and affected URLs, not vague advice.

## Check

- crawl status codes, redirects, canonicals, robots, sitemap, noindex
- titles, meta descriptions, headings, duplicate pages, thin pages
- internal links, orphan pages, broken links, anchor clarity
- structured data, Open Graph, hreflang when relevant
- search intent match and content gaps

## Output

Return:

1. SEO readiness score
2. critical blockers
3. top affected URLs or URL patterns
4. recommended fixes
5. verification method

Mark anything requiring Search Console, rank tracking, or live SERP checks as unverified until data is provided.
